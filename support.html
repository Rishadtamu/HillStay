Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?
You can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Go to My Trips, click the booking, and choose Cancel. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
Is payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed through secure encrypted channels. Your card details are never stored on our servers. We comply with PCI-DSS standards.
What is a Permit-required destination?
Some destinations in Sikkim (like Nathula Pass) require an Inner Line Permit. When you book a stay in such an area, our team will guide you through the permit application process at no extra charge.
Can I book for a group?
Absolutely! Most of our homestays can accommodate groups. You can specify the number of guests during booking. For groups of 8+, contact us directly for special group rates.
Is the local guide service reliable?
All our guides are certified, background-checked, and personally verified by the Hilligo team. Many have been with us for years and are genuine locals who love sharing their region's culture.
