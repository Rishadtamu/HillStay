About HillStay

Born in the heart of the Himalayas, dedicated to uncovering the most authentic stays in the mountains.

Our Story

HillStay was born from a simple belief: travel should be more than a place to sleep — it's about connection, culture, and the unseen beauty of the hills. We started in Darjeeling, working directly with local families who open their homes and hearts to travellers.

Today, we connect thousands of travellers with handpicked homestays across Darjeeling, Sikkim, and Nagaland. Every stay on our platform is personally verified by our local team for authenticity, comfort, and that irreplaceable warmth of mountain hospitality.

Our mission is to empower local hosts, preserve cultural heritage, and provide travellers with a "handy" tool that makes exploring the Eastern Himalayas as easy as a mountain breeze.
